Meet the Students & Staff

Learn about their appearances, powers, personalities, and interactions at the Academy.

Asa Mensah (Stormborn)

Appearance: A typical middle-schooler often seen in casual clothes like a Spiderman or Mavs hoodie, initially wearing mismatched socks and untied laces.

Ability: **Storm Affinity** — the power to generate and control electrical energy, atmospheric pressure, and weather patterns.

Personality: Sarcastic, humorous, a tech and machinery enthusiast who loves building things, and a highly reluctant hero.

Interactions: Lea's cousin and best friend, he quickly befriends CJ, Elijah, and Noah. He constantly annoys Iris.

Control Level: 1/10 — his powers manifest dramatically and uncontrollably, often in response to stress or his own jokes.

Lea Acquaye (Pink Nova)

Appearance: Fashion-conscious, with a long braid, often dressed in clothes that fit her "cute girl aesthetic". She claimed a dorm room that was entirely pink.

Ability: **Photonic Manipulation** — the ability to control light, capable of producing concentrated, white-hot spheres of pulsing plasma and heat.

Personality: Pragmatic, organized (except when late), and very protective of Asa, often serving as his voice of common sense.

Interactions: Asa's cousin and closest friend, she is often exasperated by his antics but sticks by him.

Control Level: 1/10 — initially dormant, her powers are highly volatile and triggered by proximity to Asa's escalating powers.

Iris (No-Nonsense Enforcer)

Appearance: Pale skin, straight, glossy black hair the length of her shoulder blades, a pink flower hairpin, and always carries two scimitars and a large arsenal of throwing stars.

Ability: **Mind Manipulation**.

Personality: Stern, highly efficient, and easily exasperated by students, especially Asa. She acts like she owns the place.

Interactions: Works alongside Aisha to recruit students; she is frequently annoyed by Asa's pranks and attitude.

Control Level: High — she can withstand significant attacks, such as Asa's full lightning blast, without injury.

Aisha (Cool-Headed Mentor)

Appearance: Dark skin and dark, lightly tangled curly hair that falls to the bottom of her shoulder blades; she carries a staff made of sleek, black wood carved with runes.

Ability: **Accelerated Healing** — can speed up the healing process of others with a flow of greenish-gold energy.

Personality: Calm, observant, and often amused by Asa’s antics; she’s quick to record funny moments.

Interactions: Works with Iris to recruit and mentor new students; she is more laid-back than Iris and helps Shiloh with a knee injury.

Control Level: High — demonstrates precise application of her healing abilities.

CJ (Shadow Walker)

Appearance: (Specific facial features unclear from text), carries a sword in a sheath.

Ability: **Shadow Travel** — can disappear and reappear using shadows.

Personality: Laid-back, mischievous, and quickly becomes Asa’s friend.

Interactions: Guides Asa and Lea on their first day, introduces Asa to other friends like Elijah and Noah, and dislikes Iris’s strictness.

Control Level: Moderate — proficient enough to use his power in sparring matches.

Shiloh (The Architect)

Appearance: Brown hair, brown eyes, peach skin, usually weighed down by a heavy hiking backpack filled with supplies like markers and a computer case.

Ability: Hinted at high durability/strength (hitting her knee hard without pain) and rapid healing aided by Aisha's power.

Personality: Focused, artistic (draws or builds miniature cities out of pens), and slightly accident-prone.

Interactions: Sits next to Asa in Tech and Machinery class.

Control Level: Unknown — abilities seem largely outside her conscious control.
