Abilities & Affinities

Explore the unique elemental powers of our students and staff.

Asa Mensah - Stormborn

Power: Storm Affinity

Description: Generates and controls electricity, atmospheric pressure, and weather patterns.

Control Level: 1/10 — extremely untamed, triggered by emotions or sneezes.

Quirks: Can accidentally empty vending machines, create massive lightning bolts, or trigger mini thunderstorms indoors.

Lea Acquaye - Pink Nova

Power: Photonic Manipulation

Description: Controls light and plasma, can create unstable white-hot spheres.

Control Level: 1/10 — dormant initially, volatile when triggered.

Quirks: Can flash-cook her lunch, glow unintentionally if bracelet removed.

Iris - No-Nonsense Enforcer

Power: Mind Manipulation

Description: Can influence thoughts and mental states. Highly efficient in control.

Control Level: High — able to withstand Asa’s full power attack.

Quirks: Smug, enjoys recording Asa’s mishaps.

Aisha - Cool-Headed Mentor

Power: Accelerated Healing

Description: Heals injuries instantly using greenish-gold energy.

Control Level: High — precise and careful application.

Quirks: Tech-savvy, calm under chaos, secretly slips iron to CJ to stabilize him.

CJ - Shadow Walker

Power: Shadow Travel

Description: Can disappear and travel through shadows, strike from behind.

Control Level: Moderate — loses tiny % of mass per travel.

Quirks: Mischievous, prankster, sometimes becomes partially intangible.

Shiloh - The Architect

Power: High Durability / Strength (mysterious anchor effect)

Description: Body adapts to high strain; helps stabilize the academy physically.

Control Level: Unknown — body mostly self-regulating.

Quirks: Carries massive backpack, draws miniature city models, mysterious glowing knees.
